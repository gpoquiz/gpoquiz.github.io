About Me

My name is Grant Poquiz, I am a current graduate student at the University of Texas at Dallas. I enjoy reading books, playing games, and hanging out with friends.

I've wanted to work on games for a long time. Unfortunately, I've never had the skill (or drive) for artistic pursuits. Not to say I think myself uncreative. It's just that I can't comprehend how to actualize my imagination outside of computer science. So, it's a dream of mine to be able to contribute meaningfully to a group effort, rather than to make my own solo game.

My top book series is The Stormlight Archive, an epic fantasy by Brandon Sanderson. I enjoy listening to soundtracks while I work/program. My favorite gamedev studio is Supergiant Games, who made Bastion, Transistor, Pyre, and Hades (coincidentally very good soundtracks).
