About Me

My name is Grant Poquiz, I am a former graduate student at the University of Texas at Dallas, and a current Software Developer at Paycom. I enjoy reading books, playing games, and hanging out with friends.

I've wanted to work on games for a long time. Specifically, I want to be a part of a larger effort towards greatness. So, it's a dream of mine to be able to contribute meaningfully to a large game, rather than to make my own solo game.

My top book series is The Stormlight Archive, a fantasy epic by Brandon Sanderson. I enjoy listening to soundtracks while I work/program. My favorite gamedev studio is Supergiant Games, who made Bastion, Transistor, Pyre, and Hades (Very good soundtracks).
